The project was designed to facilitate <span style='background-color:#fff5b1'>**user-centric information systems**</span>, aiming to streamline information retrieval for users, particularly new employees in need of support, and <u>to accelerate their onboarding process(this is the reason why our project name is "boarding pass".)</u> within the organization. Our team aspired to develop an <span style='background-color:#fff5b1'>**AI chatbot**</span> to create an accessible information system. Given our goal to develop a prototype, we collaborated with computer engineering students, and I assumed the role of <span style='background-color:#f5f0ff'>project manager</span> to organize diverse perspective into our project. The project successfully reached the final round, and we were able to realize the implementation of the prototype.
